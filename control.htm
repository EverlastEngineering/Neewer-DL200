Ping Pong

Move the dolly back and forth. 'End sequence' gracefully finishes the remaining movement. A 'Stop' command will end the movement early.

Super slowmode

Uses the slow acceleration setting and a repeated on/off with delay to achieve a very slow movement.

Micromovements

Minus and plus keys trigger micromovements. The movement timing is not accurate or reliably.
