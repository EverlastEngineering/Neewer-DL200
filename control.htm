Special Functions

Ping Pong

Move the dolly back and forth. 'End sequence' gracefully finishes the remaining movement. A 'Stop' command will end the movement early.

[Travel for ms] [Stop for ms]

Super slowmode

Uses the slow acceleration setting and a repeated on/off with delay to achieve a very slow movement.

[Delay factor ms]

Comma to go left, period to go right, space to stop.

Fixed duration movements

Timed movements, also suitable for micromovements. The smallest movement is not terribly accurate and occasionally the little dude will just keep on going, so caution. The minimum is roughly equal to 0.4mm.

[ In motion for ms ]

Minus and plus keys trigger fixed duration movements.
